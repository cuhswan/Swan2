Tasks we are woking on

Here are the different tasks we have to work on.We have teams according to tasks and also want you to join our society if you want to work for our nature and Earth.

Explore Society Essentials!


Volunteers will be awarded with certificate and some other perks

Bark Brigade

Objective:This team will focus on providing care and assistance to our animal friends, particularly dogs.

Med Heroes

Objective: Comprising medical professionals or volunteers with medical knowledge, this team will cater to the health needs of our furry friends.

Eco Explorers

Objective:This team, also known as the Nature Buddies, will focus on exploring and preserving the natural environment around us.

Dirt Busters

Objective:Responsible for collecting and maintaining data related to the campus dog population, including health records and behavior observations.

Trash Transformers

Objective:Brainstorming innovative ideas to repurpose waste materials into useful structures and items, such as eco-bricks.

Green Guardians

Objective: Focused on spreading awareness about environmental issues, this team will engage with students and the community through various awareness campaigns.

Data Paws

Objective:Responsible for collecting and maintaining data related to the campus dog population, including health records and behavior observations.

Flora Finders

Objective: Tasked with documenting the flora on campus, this team will conduct surveys to identify and catalog plant species and monitor their growth and health.
